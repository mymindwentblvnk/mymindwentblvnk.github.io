Simple Pasta Dough

A basic pasta dough recipe with just three ingredients.

Prep time: 15 minutes
Cook time: 0 minutes
Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 500g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 spoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Create a well in the center and add the eggs.
  3. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for 10 minutes.
  4. Rest for 30 minutes before rolling out.
